Analysis of Lattitude and Weather

A random sample of over 500 cities in the world was gathered. Weather data for 8/12/2022 was obtained for these cities using the Open Weather Map API. The data points were analyzed to identify if any correlations exist in the various points of interest and the city's distance from the equator (latitude).

Weather Data (points of interest):
  • Max Temperature (Degrees F)
  • Humidity (%)
  • Cloudiness (%)
  • Wind Speed (mi/hr)
Limitiations
  • This data is a snapshot in time covering only one day in August. The data if gathered over a longer timespan or even if taken on a day during a different time of the year, may show different results.
